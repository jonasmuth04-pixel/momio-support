❓ FAQ
How do I create an event?
Tap the plus icon, give your event a name, set the time window, and share the invitation link with your friends.
How do guests join?
Via invitation link or QR code. Joining is always free — no subscription needed.
Are photos shared automatically?
Yes! After granting photo library access, photos taken during the event are uploaded automatically to the shared album.
What is Momio Pro?
Momio Pro unlocks unlimited events, more members per event, and extra features. Available as a monthly or yearly subscription.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone. You can manage or cancel your Momio subscription there at any time.
