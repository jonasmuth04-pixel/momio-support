Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Momio ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Momio mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.

Photos and Videos: When you join an event, the app accesses photos and videos from your device's camera roll that were taken during the event time window. These are uploaded to shared event albums.

Device Information: We collect device tokens for push notifications.

Event Data: Event names, invitation links, and QR codes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your photos and videos with event albums
  • To send push notifications about event updates
  • To manage your subscription

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. Photos and videos are stored in encrypted cloud storage. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Photos and videos you upload are shared only with other members of the same event.

5. Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication, database, and photo storage. Supabase stores data on secure servers.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications

7. Photo Library Access

Momio requires access to your photo library to automatically sync photos taken during events. We only access photos within the event's time range. We never access photos outside of event time windows.

8. Children's Privacy

Momio is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Data Security

We use technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
